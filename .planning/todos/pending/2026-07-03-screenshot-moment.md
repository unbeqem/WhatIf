## Problem

Nothing nudges users to capture/share the result — free distribution left on the
table.

## Solution

A tasteful load moment (brief glow/pulse sweep when the result mounts) and a
subtly framed result zone that invites a screenshot. Keep it restrained — no
gaudy confetti. Respect prefers-reduced-motion.
